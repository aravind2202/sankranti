About the Festival

Makar Sankranti is a major harvest festival celebrated across India. It marks the transition of the Sun into the zodiac sign of Makara (Capricorn).
It is a day of joy, kite flying, and delicious traditional sweets.

Festival Rituals & Food

Morning Rituals (Ordered List)
